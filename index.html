Vision Statement

LexLiberty envisions a world where the radiant light of Christian values illuminates the path of every child, guiding them towards a future defined by unwavering faith, compassion, and moral fortitude.

Mission Statement

Our mission at LexLiberty is to craft a nurturing haven that fosters a profound comprehension of Christian teachings, empowering children to embody compassion and principled character. Through our innovative Children's Christian Center, Music Studio for Recording Christian Songs, and various initiatives, we are dedicated to inspiring, educating, and equipping young minds with the tools essential for a purposeful Christian life.

Our Goals

Music Studio for Recording Christian Songs

- Establish a music studio dedicated to recording and promoting Christian music.

- Identify and support talented children, offering them a platform to serve God through their musical gifts.

Contact Us

Creative Christian Music Studio for Free Training

- Develop an educational program covering piano, guitar, violin, vocals, solfeggio, drawing, and modeling.

- Ensure free access to lessons and tools, supporting children's creative development through diverse forms of art.

Contact Us

Relief Fund for Former Prisoners of Christian

- Provide financial and moral support to Christian prisoners and their families.

- Collect and share real stories of those who suffered during the Soviet Union, fostering awareness and understanding.

Contact Us

Children's Christian Center

- Develop educational programs fostering intellectual, creative, and spiritual growth in preschool and school-age children.

- Provide religious studies delving into the Bible and Christian history to instill foundational faith and a Christian worldview.

- Cultivate creative activities to unveil and nurture children's artistic potential.

Contact Us

Christian Sunday School

- Formulate a program to instill spiritual and Christian values in children.

- Create training sessions focusing on the development of human and personal qualities in a supportive and inspiring environment.
